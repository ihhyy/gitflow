Apple MacBook Air M4 (2025)


Name


Apple MacBook Air M4 Chip 13" is the latest MacBook Air model from Apple's 2025 lineup featuring the advanced M4 chip. It combines powerful performance with sleek portability, ideal for students, professionals, and general users seeking a lightweight yet high-performance laptop.


Description


The MacBook Air 2025 with Apple M4 chip represents the pinnacle of ultraportable laptops. It delivers fanless, silent performance, extended battery life, and advanced macOS features. Its slim profile, powerful CPU, GPU, and Neural Engine make it suitable for creative, professional, and everyday tasks while maintaining portability.


Specifications


Processor: Apple M4 with 10-core CPU, 8- or 10-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine for AI tasks.

Display: 13.6-inch Liquid Retina, 2560x1664 resolution, 500 nits brightness, True Tone, 1 billion colors.

Memory: 16 GB unified memory (configurable to 24 GB or 32 GB).

Storage: 256 GB SSD (configurable up to 2 TB).

Battery & Life: Built-in lithium-polymer, up to 18 hours streaming video.

Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, 2x Thunderbolt 4/USB-C, MagSafe 3, 3.5mm headphone jack.

Weight: ~1.24 kg (2.7 lbs).

Dimensions: 0.44 in x 11.97 in x 8.46 in.
